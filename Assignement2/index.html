General writing :-

Technical writing aims to be extremely clear and precise, often using straightforward language and avoiding ambiguity. General writing may be more flexible in style and can sometimes prioritize creativity over precision.
Technical writing is highly focused on understanding and catering to a specific audience with specialized knowledge or needs. General writing may target a broader audience with varying levels of expertise or interests.
Technical writing places a strong emphasis on accuracy, ensuring that information is factually correct and free from bias. General writing may allow for more subjective expression and storytelling.
